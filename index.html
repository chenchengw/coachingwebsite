Career & Leadership Coaching: Thrive in Uncertainty

For professionals and leaders feeling stretched by pressure, change, and rising expectations. Whether you're preparing for a promotion, managing a difficult transition, or trying to balance ambition with life outside of work, I help you find a path forward that is thoughtful, practical, and aligned with who you are.

Best for: leaders, managers, and high-achievers facing transitions or uncertainty.

For leaders and professionals who want an experienced, objective thought partner. I help you step back, see situations more clearly, and make better decisions. Best for: anyone who wants practical, grounded guidance without a long-term coaching engagement.

About Me

I help ambitious professionals navigate uncertainty and define success on their own terms.

I have over a decade of leadership experience in high-growth technology companies—from pre-IPO hyper-growth to mature public-company scale—helping teams navigate change, scale quickly, and deliver meaningful impact. My career has combined engineering leadership, organizational design, and business strategy.

Most recently, as an Engineering Director at Instacart, I led 50+ engineers across growth and shopping, helping drive double-digit growth in challenging market conditions. Before that, at LinkedIn, I helped build and scale a B2B product from the ground up into a billion-dollar business.

Along the way, I was promoted five times in ten years—while raising two children and navigating the added complexity of being a woman and minority leader in a male-dominated industry. I learned firsthand how to balance ambition with wellbeing, lead through uncertainty, and grow without burning out. Those experiences shape how I coach today.
